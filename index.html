Questions

Frequently asked.

Common questions about how Islam Focus works.

Is Islam Focus free?

Yes. Islam Focus is completely free to download and use. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no premium tiers. We built it as a sadaqah jariyah — a continuous charity — for the Ummah.

Does Islam Focus track what I do on my phone?

No. Islam Focus only detects when an app you've personally chosen to limit opens. We do not read your screen, your messages, your keystrokes, or anything else. Your data stays on your device — not on our servers.

Why does the app need Accessibility permission?

Android's Accessibility Service is the only way for an app to detect when another app opens. We use it strictly for this purpose — to show you a mindful pause when you open a distracting app. The permission is fully transparent and you can revoke it any time from your phone's settings.

Will this work on iPhone?

We're currently focused on launching for Android. iOS has stricter limitations around app-blocking due to Apple's policies. We're exploring an iOS version using Screen Time APIs — sign up for updates to be the first to know.

Can I choose which apps to limit?

Yes. You have full control. During setup, you choose exactly which apps trigger the intervention. You can change this list anytime in Settings.

What if I really need to use a limited app?

After the brief pause, you can always choose to continue. There's also a 30-second grace period after you tap "Continue" so you can use the app without repeated interruptions. Islam Focus is a gentle guide, not a strict prison.

Who made Islam Focus?

Islam Focus is built by SAP-SHARKS — a small team of Muslim developers who believe technology should bring us closer to Allah, not farther. You can reach us anytime at support@sapsharks.com.
