Why is iOS taking longer?

iOS has stricter privacy rules than Android, for very good reasons. Apple doesn't allow background app blocking the way Android does. We're carefully designing an iOS version using Apple's Screen Time API, Focus Filters, and Family Controls framework to give you the same Dhikr-and-Quran intervention experience while respecting Apple's privacy model.

FAQ

Questions about iOS

When will the iOS version launch?

We're targeting a launch within the next 2-4 months, inshaAllah. We want to make sure it's properly tested and meets Apple's strict App Store review guidelines before releasing.

Why can't iOS work exactly like Android?

iOS has stricter privacy rules. Apple doesn't allow apps to detect which specific app is opening (a privacy protection). Instead, iOS uses Apple's Screen Time API, Family Controls, and DeviceActivity framework. The end-user experience will feel the same, but the technical approach is different.

Will iOS have the same features as Android?

Yes! Dhikr Mode, Quran Mode, customizable pause duration, theme colors, and streak tracking will all be available. Some technical differences may exist (like using app categories instead of individual apps), but the spiritual benefit will be the same.

Will it be free like Android?

Yes. Heemu on iOS will be completely free, just like the Android version. No ads, no in-app purchases. It's built as sadaqah jariyah for the Ummah.

Can I use the Android version on my iPhone?

Unfortunately no. Android apps don't run on iPhone. You'll need to wait for the dedicated iOS version. Join the waitlist above to get notified when it's ready.

Is there a beta program?

We'll be running a TestFlight beta closer to launch. Waitlist members will get first access. Make sure to sign up above!
